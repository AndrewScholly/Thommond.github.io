My mission

I want to help society forward. I want to see my change in the making. I want to run for mayor. Unlike most jobs you can have an impact on everyone in society directly. I want to run for mayor to spread the belief of liberty, freedom, and the pursue of happness. If I every have the prevlidge to be a mayor I hope to give people insperation about the future and let others decied their path.

Why Mayor?

I know that the responsibility of congress and governer may overwhelm me for my first public service. I want to effect a small group of people so I can focus on their problems to make life easier for them. I can solve the problems the few which is just as important as serving the needs of the many. When you are mayor people thank you face to face or tell you their struggles and you can truly connect.

Why me?

Why am I worthy of being a mayor? I cant say I am the best or that I am the one to change the town better than ever before. However, That is my goal. I want to stick up for the indivdual and support the people at ever possible every turn. Together is how we change for the better.
