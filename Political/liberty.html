The N.A.P

I full support the N.A.P. which stands for the Non-Aggression principle. What this means is anyone who shows acts of aggrssion towards others may violate the N.A.P. This does not mean just if you get angry, this means anyone who puts merit behide their aggression.For example, Assault, theft, violation of contract, etc. Want to know a bit more? Here is aWikapidia page about the N.A.P.

Gun policy

Libertarians including myself support the 2nd amendment in its entirety. I support americans right to defend themselves no matter the circumstances. No gun bans, no gun restrictions, no gun regulations, no gun attachment bans and finally no bullshit. I take our rights very seriously.

Drug policy

Many libertarians such as myself support decriminalization of all drugs and the legalization of cannabis as well as Psycoactive mushrooms. I support this because in all reality no matter if a substance is illegal those who want it will do it anyway. For example the prohibition of alchohol in the 1920s. My point is prohibiton of any substance does not work.
